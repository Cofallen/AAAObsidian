# 周复盘

> [!abstract] 本周寄语
> 

---

> [!summary]+ 📅 每日计划（按天）
> ```dataview
> LIST rows.L.text
> FROM "MyLife/Daily"
> FLATTEN file.lists as L
> WHERE contains(meta(L.section).subpath, "每日计划")
> AND file.day >= date(this.file.name, "kkkk-'W'WW")
> AND file.day <= date(this.file.name, "kkkk-'W'WW") + dur(6 days)
> GROUP BY file.link
> SORT file.day asc
> ```
