> TASK
> FROM "MyLife/Daily"
> WHERE contains(meta(section).subpath, "每日计划")
> AND file.day >= date(this.file.name, "kkkk-'W'WW")
> AND file.day <= date(this.file.name, "kkkk-'W'WW") + dur(6 days)
> SORT file.day asc
> GROUP BY file.link
> ```

> [!summary]+ 📚 本周知识整理
> ```dataview
> LIST rows.L.text
> FROM "MyLife/Daily"
> FLATTEN date(this.file.name, "kkkk-'W'WW") as week_start
> WHERE file.day >= week_start
> AND file.day <= week_start + dur(6 days)
> FLATTEN file.lists as L
> WHERE contains(meta(L.section).subpath, "今日知识整理")

> ```
